Site Plan: Gym Experts

1. Site Identification

Site Name: Gym Experts

Logo: (My friend hasn't provided me with a logo yet)

Description: An intuitive platform designed for people looking to start at the gym or who already have experience. I used the name because the routines will be provided by two gym experts, which also makes people more confident in the routines.

Domain Availability (Optional): gymexperts.online

2. Site Purpose

The website acts as a management hub for the user, providing:

  • A searchable library of exercises categorized by muscle group.
  • An interactive tool to create and save personalized routines.
  • A progress tracking system using localStorage so that information persists between sessions.

3. Scenarios

  • Beginner User: "What exercises can I do today to work my chest and triceps without needing specialized equipment?" (The site will respond by filtering exercises by muscle group and equipment.)
  • Recurring User: "How was the weight or the reps I did in my last squat session?" (The site will display the browser's saved history.)
